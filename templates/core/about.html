About NAAS

Our Vision

To be the foremost student association that champions academic excellence, cultural preservation, and community development among Ankpa indigenes in higher institutions across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

To unite all Ankpa students under one umbrella, foster unity, promote education, preserve our cultural heritage, and contribute positively to the development of our community.

Our Objectives

  • To promote unity and brotherhood among Ankpa students
  • To encourage academic excellence and scholarship
  • To preserve and promote Ankpa cultural heritage
  • To provide welfare support for members
  • To contribute to community development projects
  • To foster leadership and personal development

Our History

The National Association of Ankpa Students (NAAS) was established to bring together students from Ankpa Local Government Area studying in various institutions across Nigeria. Over the years, NAAS has grown to become a respected voice for Ankpa students, organizing various programs and initiatives that benefit both members and the Ankpa community at large.
